Sign Up

The First Step is to Sign Up on the Web Portal.

Use your correct details in the signup.

In Case of any Issues, Contact us

Login

When you open the app for the first time, you’ll see a Login button.

Please note that only members of ADG are allowed on this platform.

Use the Account credentials you filled in while signing up on the Web Portal to Login. If you face any issues regarding the same Contact Us

Home

Welcome to the Home Screen of the ADG Connect app.

This is where you can find information about each domain and quickly glance over upcoming meetings and tasks under Notifications.

Alerts

Alerts page holds all your Meetings and Tasks.

All your upcoming meetings and tasks are well organised in this section of the app.

You can Acknowledge for a meeting that you will be joining or you can post a reason for your Unavailablity.

Acknowledgement

There are two buttons under each Meeting or Task posted in the Alert Section.

Please Note that you can only choose one option.

Clicking on Acknowledge means that you’re taking part in the Meeting or Task.

Clicking on Unavailable will open a popup. You should post your reasons for being unavailable here.

MOM

Minutes of Meeting or MOM are posted under this section.

Click on any meeting in the list to open the MOM.

Here you can find a short summary of what the meeting was called for and the points discussed.

Profile

Your profile details and the teams that you are a part of are displayed here.

In case of any changes or updates do let us know.

If you choose to Reset your Password, a link is sent to the Registered E-Mail ID to do the same.
